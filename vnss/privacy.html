Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 23, 2026

Short version: We collect very little data. We don't sell it, share it for profit, or use it to target you with ads. What we do collect is used only to run multiplayer and understand how the game performs.

1. WHO WE ARE

Very Normal Store Simulator is developed and published by BunnE Games, a solo indie game studio. You can reach us at bunnrbbt@gmail.com.

This policy explains what data is collected when you play Very Normal Store Simulator on PC (via Steam, Epic Games Store, or itch.io), how it is used, and what rights you have.

2. DATA WE COLLECT

Platform account data (Steam / Epic Games)

To play multiplayer, the game authenticates you through the platform you purchased it on — either Valve's Steam or Epic Online Services (EOS). This gives us access to:

  • Your platform user ID (a numeric or alphanumeric identifier, not your email)
  • Your platform display name (your Steam name or Epic display name)

This data is provided by Steam or Epic and is used only to identify you in multiplayer sessions, display your name to other players, and maintain session state. We do not store this data on our own servers beyond the duration of an active session.

Analytics and crash data

The game may collect anonymous or pseudonymous telemetry data to help us understand how it performs and how players interact with it. This may include:

  • Crash reports and error logs (including device specs and OS version at time of crash)
  • Gameplay events (e.g. levels reached, features used, session length)
  • Performance metrics (frame rate, load times)

This data is collected by a third-party analytics provider. [PROVIDER NAME — update before launch] It is not linked to your real identity. You can opt out of analytics data collection in the game's settings menu.

3. DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT

  • Your email address
  • Your real name
  • Your location beyond what your platform account may expose
  • Payment or billing information (all purchases are handled by Steam, Epic, or itch.io)
  • Any data from children — this game is not directed at children under 13

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Very Normal Store Simulator integrates with the following third-party platforms. Each has its own privacy policy which governs how they handle your data:

  • Valve / Steam — multiplayer authentication and social features for Steam purchases. See Steam's Privacy Policy.
  • Epic Online Services (EOS) — multiplayer authentication for Epic Games Store and itch.io purchases. See Epic's Privacy Policy.
  • itch.io — storefront for PC purchases. See itch.io's Privacy Policy.
  • [Analytics provider] — crash reporting and gameplay analytics. [Link to their privacy policy]

BunnE Games does not control these third parties and is not responsible for their data practices. We encourage you to review their policies.

5. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We use the data described above solely to:

  • Enable multiplayer functionality (authentication, session management, display names)
  • Identify and fix bugs and crashes
  • Understand how the game is played so we can improve it

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with any party beyond the third-party services listed in Section 4.

6. DATA RETENTION

Session-related platform data (your user ID and display name during multiplayer) is not stored by us after your session ends. Analytics and crash data is retained by our analytics provider according to their own retention policies, typically between 90 days and 2 years.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on where you live, you may have rights over your personal data. In particular, if you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

Because most data is handled by Steam, Epic, or our analytics provider, requests related to that data should be directed to those services. For anything handled directly by BunnE Games, contact us at bunnrbbt@gmail.com.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy as the game develops — particularly once we confirm our analytics provider. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
