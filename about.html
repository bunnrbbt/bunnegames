BunnE Games

a one-bunny indie studio

BunnE Games is one bunny. One person, one keyboard, and a deeply unreasonable amount of coffee — making small, strange, heartfelt games that nobody asked for and a few people will love a lot.

The whole thing runs on a simple promise: no lootboxes, no microtransactions, no dark patterns. No "engagement loops," no daily login bribes, no spreadsheets wearing a game as a costume. Just things that are fun to make and fun to play.

Everything here is built slowly, by hand, in the gaps between sleep and sense. Some of it will be weird. Some of it will be broken in interesting ways. All of it is made with love & chaos, in roughly equal parts.

If a game from this studio ever makes you smile on a bad day, then it did its whole job. That's the entire business plan.

Want to say hi, send a bug, or just yell into the void? The inbox is always open: bunnrbbt@gmail.com
